\section{Key Activities}

Key Activities describes the most important things a company must do to make its business model work.

\subsection{Process}

Using agile based development process, there are few main processes:

\begin{easylist}
& Product planning
& Designing interface
& Developing implementation
& Change and improvement
& Iteration
\end{easylist}

\subsection{Build}

\begin{easylist}
& Minimum Viable and Lovable Product (MVLP)
& Software design and development
& User Acceptance Testing (UAT)
& User revalidation
& Growth hacking
\end{easylist}

\subsection{Production}

The system/platform should always be:

\begin{easylist}
& Built/Coded
& Delivered/Shipped
& Supported/Maintained
\end{easylist}

\subsection{Problem Solving}

\begin{easylist}
& Agile refinement and research
\end{easylist}

\subsection{Network}

\begin{easylist}
& Growing the network
& Answering and discussing questions and feedbacks
\end{easylist}

\subsection{Payment}

\begin{easylist}
& Paying resources
& Paying taxes
\end{easylist}
